Pakistan Forest Trees Carbon Calculator

About This Calculator

The Pakistan Forest Trees Carbon Calculator is a scientifically developed tool designed to estimate above-ground biomass (AGB), carbon stock, and carbon sequestration potential of forest tree species. The model uses species-specific allometric equations based on measurable tree parameters such as Diameter at Breast Height (DBH) and tree height. It supports applications in climate change studies, carbon accounting, forestry management, and environmental monitoring.

Pakistan Trees Carbon Calculator

The Pakistan Forest Trees Carbon Calculator is based on tree species-specific allometric equations developed by Dr. Sami Ullah (Dr.rer.nat.), Associate Professor of Digital Forestry and Chairman, Department of Forestry and Wildlife Management, The University of Haripur, Khyber Pukhtunkhwa, Pakistan.

Dr. Sami Ullah also serves as Co-Principal Investigator (Co-PI) in the Forest Resource Assessment Section, G-SAB Lab, National Center of GIS and Space Application.
